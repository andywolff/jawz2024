About the Couple vertical_align_top

Jenny and Andy found each other at Worcester Polytechnic Institute through mutual connections. Jenny knew Andy as the elusive roommate who would only pop out to school everyone at Mario Kart. Andy knew Jenny as Bethany's cute friend with the contagious giggle.

They gradually grew closer, building classic memories such as guessing Andy's middle name (Andrew Andrew Wolff of course!) and waking Jenny up to the smell of fresh banana bread under her nose. Andy survived an attempt at skateboarding down a parking lot ramp. He failed to stay up, but learned how to stop.

After graduating, Andy invited Jenny and a few other friends to celebrate the 4th of July at one of his favorite places in the world, his grandmother's pond-side home in Maine. This was Jenny's first road trip with her new car, Teddy. Andy kept her calm through a sudden hailstorm on the drive up. The weekend was filled with laughs and fun times by the firepit, especially after Andy's mom started mixing margaritas for everyone. Fireworks sparked over the pond, and also in their hearts. For the next few weeks, Andy and Jenny were flirtatiously inseparable.

On July 20th, 2014, they agreed they were dating. They spent the next 10 years laughing, traveling, growing, shrinking their waistlines, beefing their muscles, and watching their lives intertwine happily. On their 10-year anniversary, they will celebrate their love together as their friends and family witness this sickeningly cute couple exchange their vows.
